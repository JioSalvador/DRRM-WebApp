International Students Registration Guidelines

Updated Policy: These guidelines specify required documents for Resident & Non-Resident International Students to ensure compliance with institutional and immigration rules.

Walk-In Reactivation
  1. Request or download the form at DRRM Office (Window D).
  2. Get SAO and Accounting clearances.
  3. Return to Window D for reactivation.
  4. Enroll via UE iEnroll.
Online Reactivation
  1. Email: ienroll.cal@ue.edu.ph
  2. Fill out the form and wait for confirmation.
  3. Once reactivated, proceed to UE iEnroll.
  4. Pay at the Cashier and print your Registration Invoice.
