Student Verifications
Step-by-Step Process

This is the step-by-step process for companies applying for student verification.

Note

Verifying companies that submitted their verification online may still claim the hard copy of the verification result.

Requirements
Online Applications
  • Scanned Transcript of Records
  • Valid ID of Student
  • Student Consent (emailed by DRRM)
  • Company ID of Verifier
Walk-in Applications
  • Photocopy of Transcript of Records
  • Valid ID of Student
  • Student Consent (emailed by DRRM)
  • Company ID of Verifier
Fees & Payment
Local Fee: ₱150
Fee Abroad: $5
