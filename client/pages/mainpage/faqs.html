Credentials-related FAQs

You can request your transcript by submitting a request form to DRRM associated with the transcript request from current school.
Requirements:
- Request letter from current school

You need to present an affidavit of loss and request letter from your current school.
Requirements:
- Affidavit of loss, Request letter from school

Transfer-Out FAQs

To transfer, you must not be currently enrolled or you dropped your subjects, and apply for an honorable dismissal, transcript of records for evaluation (optional) and provide a request letter from the current school if available

Other FAQs

Visit DRRM and provide necessary documents
Requirements:
- ID of any relatives residing at your current address, personal letter (reason and purpose of change of address), Student ID, Brgy. Certificate
